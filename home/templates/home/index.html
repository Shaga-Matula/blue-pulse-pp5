Blue Pulse

Blue Pulse is a collaboration of a talented duo with a mastery of their respective instruments, Paul "fingers" Gleeson & Daren "vocal vandal" Hines, that specializes in rock and blues-based music.

The band was formed in 1998 and has since been dedicated to creating music that is both authentic and true to their influences while also showcasing their unique sound. The band draws inspiration from blues legends such as Led Zeppelin, Rory Gallagher, Muddy Waters, BB King, Gary Moore, and Ken Dod.

The band's rock/blues-based music is produced, mixed, and recorded at Shagamatula Studios International in both London, England, and the Republic of Ireland, using Cubase: Music Production Software - DAW. Blue Pulse is always looking for new ways to incorporate techniques into their music to create something fresh and exciting in a bluesy fashion.

As a collaboration of two great musical talents, Blue Pulse is able to create a sound that is both powerful and unique while staying true to the blues and rock genres. The band's dedication to their craft and their love for rock/blues-based music is evident in every note they play.
