Blue Pulse


Blue Pulse is a collaboration of a talented duo with a mastery of their respective instruments, Paul "Fingers" Gleeson & Daren "The Belter" Hines, that specializes in rock and blues-based music.

The ensemble was established in 1998 and has steadfastly committed itself to the creation of music that remains faithful to its influences while simultaneously highlighting its distinctive sonic signature. The band draws inspiration from esteemed blues luminaries, including but not limited to Rory Gallagher, Muddy Waters, BB King, Gary Moore, and Joe Cocker.

The band's rock/blues-based music is produced, mixed, and recorded at Shagamatula Studios International in both London, England, and the South of Ireland, using Cubase: Music Production Software - DAW. Blue Pulse is always looking for new ways to incorporate techniques into their music to create something fresh and exciting in a bluesy fashion.

As a collaboration of two great musical talents, Blue Pulse is able to create a sound that is both powerful and unique while staying true to the blues and rock genres. The band's dedication to their craft and their love for rock/blues-based music is evident in every note they play.
